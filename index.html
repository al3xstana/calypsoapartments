Our Luxury Apartments

Located in Kalamaki, 2.8 km from Crystal Beach and 2.6 km from Agios Dionysios Church, Calypso Apartments provides spacious air-conditioned accommodation with a terrace and free WiFi. This property offers access to a balcony and free private parking. The property is non-smoking and is situated 2.7 km from Port of Zakynthos.

First Floor Apartment

A two bedroom apartment with fully equipped kitchen, a flat-screen TV, a seating area and one bathroom. The property offers garden views.

Guests can relax in the garden at the property.

Ground Floor Apartment

It's located on elevated floor with stairs access, and comprises three bedrooms, a large open plan lounge with a living room, modern kitchen fully equipped and two full bathrooms with shower and bathtub.

Guests can enjoy the privacy of the whole apartment which is professionally cleaned before each arrival and fully equipped.
